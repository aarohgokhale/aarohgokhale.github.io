My Reading List for 2025

January 1, 2025 · 1 min

Starting this year, I have decided to make a list of books that I will try to read and write a review of by the end of the year. Expect to see reviews for most if not all of these in 2025.

Books in English

Fiction

  • Crime and Punishment, Fyodor Dostoyevsky (I already started this, but I'm not that far in)
  • The Divine Comedy, Dante Alighieri
  • The Metamorphosis, Franz Kafka
  • The Call of Cthulhu, H.P. Lovecraft
  • Neuromancer, William Gibson
  • Snow Crash, Neal Stephenson
  • War and Peace, Leo Tolstoy

Non-Fiction

  • Guns, Germs, and Steel, Jared Diamond
  • How to Win Friends and Influence People, Dale Carnegie
  • Beyond Good and Evil, Friedrich Nietzsche
  • A Treatist of Human Nature, David Hume
  • Fear and Trembling, Søren Kierkegaard
  • Behave: The Biology of Humans at Our Best and Worst, Robert Sapolsky
  • Evariste Galois 1811–1832, Laura Toti Rigatelli
  • Leonhard Euler: Mathematical Genius in the Englightenment, Ronald S. Calinger
